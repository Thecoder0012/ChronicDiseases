More info about this site

This site was built for diagnosing the diseases diabetes, COPD and cancer. These diseases can be difficult to diagnose, as they share some common symptoms. This site is therefore tasked with discovering the symptoms of online patients and help both patients, and hospitals with finding these diseases quicker and hopefully in the early stages. Therefore, the doctors will be more prepared when they call the patients for a physical check-up, because of the information this site provides them.
